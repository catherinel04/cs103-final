About Me

Hi, I'm Catherine

I am currently a junior at Boston University majoring in Computer Science and Mathematics with a minor in Innovation and Entrepreneurship. Outside of the academic classroom, I love dance and reconnecting with parts of my culture. I have always loved listening to music in my free time, when I'm studying, and when I'm walking down the streets. I used to listen to a lot of JPOP back in 2020, but in the past few years I have transitioned into the world of Kpop!

Top Genre

My most listened to genre is kpop, or korean pop.

Kpop is a popular music genre that originated in South Korea and is known for its style-bending, aesthetic-driven, and trendsetting sound. K-pop is characterized by its wide range of influences, including pop, hip-hop, R&B, electronic, and dance, as well as traditional Korean music. It is also known for its emphasis on dance choreography and overall stage prescence.
